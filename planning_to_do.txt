Tables DB

1. Users
UserID (PK), Name, Email (Unique), Password, Phone, Role (guest/user/vendor/admin), CreatedAt, UpdatedAt

2. Buses
BusID (PK), BusName, VendorID (FK), Type, RegistrationNo, Status (Active/Inactive)

3. Routes
RouteID (PK), Source, Destination, Distance, Duration

4. Bus_Schedules
ScheduleID (PK), BusID (FK), RouteID (FK), TravelDate, DepartureTime, ArrivalTime, Fare

5. Bookings
BookingID (PK), UserID (FK), BusID (FK), TravelDate, SeatNumbers, Status (Booked/Cancelled), PaymentID (FK), BookedAt

6. Payments
PaymentID, BookingID, Amount, PaymentMethod, PaymentStatus

7. Seat Availability
BusID, TravelDate, AvailableSeats, TotalSeats

8. Vendor (Partner) Collection
vendorID, Name, Email, buses_managed, status

9. Feedback/Reviews
ReviewID, UserID, BusID, Rating, Comment, CreatedAt
